About ArchSpace Studio

ArchSpace Studio was founded in 2014 with a simple purpose: to make architecture that responds to place and people with equal care.

Our story

What began as a two-person practice focused on experimental residential projects grew into a multi-disciplinary studio. Across urban and rural commissions, the practice has kept a consistent method — a deep reading of site, pragmatic detailing, and a rigorous focus on occupant comfort.

Our portfolio spans sensitive conservation projects, low-energy homes and civic buildings. We believe that constraints spur creativity; we often take on complex sites and ambiguous briefs precisely because we see them as generative.

People

Our team brings together architects, landscape designers, model-makers, and sustainability consultants. We value diverse perspectives and maintain a studio culture that balances craft-based learning and research-led practice.

  • Lead Architect: Aria S. — design director with a background in conservation and community projects.
  • Senior Architect: Mateo R. — focused on technical delivery and material innovation.
  • Sustainability Lead: Priya K. — specialized in low-energy retrofits and lifecycle analysis.

Design philosophy

  1. Context first: We start with the site and the people.
  2. Rigour in craft: Detailing is non-negotiable.
  3. Longevity: Material and systems thinking ensure enduring value.
